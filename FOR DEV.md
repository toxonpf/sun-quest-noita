### Example
>[!multi-column]
>>[!question|min-0] Find determinant:
>>$$
>>A=\begin{pmatrix}
>>2 & 1 \\
>>3 & 4
>>\end{pmatrix}
>>$$
>
>>[!ex|wide-2] Solution:
>>
>>$$
>>\det A=\begin{vmatrix}
>>{\color{rcyan}2} & {\color{rviol}1} \\
>>{\color{rviol}3} & {\color{rcyan}4}
>>\end{vmatrix}={\color{rcyan}2\cdot 4  }-{\color{rviol}1\cdot 3  }=8-3=5
>>$$
